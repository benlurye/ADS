Brooklyn-based Interior Designer Isabella Ackerman has has led, consulted, and managed the design of living and working spaces in New York, DC, Maryland, Virginia, Florida, and California. A graduate of The George Washington University in Washington DC, Izzy has worked alongside top designers in the nation's capital and New York City.

Izzy's husband describes her as “creative without bounds...able to maximize the beauty and comfort of any space, anywhere.” She's also a really great cook and enjoys finding the best restaurants to try, has great taste in shoes, and loves going on adventures both planned and impromptu. She's also cute AF.

Her design asthetic will be described here. It's pretty baller but I want her to say it in her own words.
