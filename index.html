Isabella Ackerman earned her degree in Interior Design from The George Washington University in Washington, DC and quickly began working for top designers in the nation's capital and New York City. Now based in Brooklyn, Izzy has managed, led, and consulted on projects in New York, DC, Maryland, Virginia, Florida, and California.

Izzy's husband describes her as “creative without bounds...able to maximize the beauty and comfort of any space, anywhere.” She's also a really great cook and enjoys finding the best restaurants to try, has great taste in shoes, and loves going on adventures both planned and impromptu.
